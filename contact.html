Frequently Asked Questions

Is space travel safe?

At Stellar Expeditions, safety is our top priority. Our spacecraft are equipped with the latest safety technologies, and our crew undergoes rigorous training. We also provide comprehensive pre-flight training for all travelers.

What should I pack for my expedition?

We'll provide a detailed packing list based on your chosen expedition. Generally, you'll need comfortable clothing, personal items, and any medications. Most equipment and specialized gear are provided.

How do I prepare physically?

Our expeditions include a pre-flight fitness program tailored to space travel. We recommend moderate exercise and consultation with your doctor prior to booking.
